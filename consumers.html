FAQs

Frequently asked questions

Common consumer questions about complaints, tariffs, service providers, and BOCRA support.

How do I file a complaint with BOCRA?

You can submit a complaint through the complaint form above and attach supporting evidence where necessary.

Can I track the status of my complaint?

Yes. Complaint references can be used to track progress and view the latest handling status.

Where can I find tariff updates?

Tariff-related notices and service alerts can be published under the Tarrifs Alerts section.

What documents should I include with a complaint?

Include any bills, screenshots, correspondence, reference numbers, or supporting evidence relevant to the issue.
