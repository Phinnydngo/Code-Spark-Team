Our Mandate

Understanding BOCRA’s legal authority, regulatory scope, and core functions in guiding Botswana’s communications ecosystem.

Legal Framework

Authority grounded in law and policy

BOCRA’s regulatory powers are exercised within a formal legal and policy framework that enables fair oversight, sector development, and public accountability.

Communications Act

Provides the legislative basis for BOCRA’s authority, responsibilities, and sector oversight across communications services.

Regulatory Framework

Establishes the policies, licensing models, obligations, and compliance standards used to regulate communications markets.

International Agreements

Supports alignment with regional and international obligations relevant to communications, spectrum, and digital cooperation.

Regulatory Scope

Oversight across four communications pillars

BOCRA regulates four key sectors that support Botswana’s connectivity, public communication, digital trust, and service delivery.

Internet

Oversight of internet governance, cybersecurity support, domain-related coordination, and digital infrastructure resilience.

Broadcasting

Regulation of broadcasting services, technical compliance, broadcasting standards, and content-related obligations.

Telecommunications

Regulation of network operators, spectrum usage, service quality, competition conditions, and consumer-facing telecom services.

Postal Services

Oversight of postal operators, universal service considerations, and service standards related to mail and delivery services.

Key Functions

Core regulatory responsibilities

BOCRA’s work is delivered through practical regulatory functions that support trust, service delivery, and sector discipline.

Licensing

Issuing, renewing, and managing licences for regulated operators and communications-related service providers.

Spectrum Management

Allocating and monitoring spectrum resources to support efficient, lawful, and interference-managed communications use.

Consumer Protection

Protecting end users through complaint handling, fair practice oversight, transparency, and service quality measures.

Monitoring & Enforcement

Tracking compliance, market conduct, and adherence to regulatory obligations, with action where standards are not met.

Quality Standards

Establishing and monitoring service performance standards to improve reliability, fairness, and sector accountability.

Dispute Resolution

Supporting structured resolution of sector disputes involving operators, consumers, and compliance-related matters.

Mandate at a glance

BOCRA’s mandate combines legal authority, sector-wide oversight, and practical regulatory functions to support a trusted and well-governed communications environment in Botswana.
