BOCRA's Regulatory Pillars

BOCRA regulates and oversees four critical sectors essential to Botswana's digital and communications infrastructure.

Telecommunications

Licensing, spectrum management, quality of service standards, and consumer protection in the telecommunications sector.

Broadcasting

Content regulation, licensing for broadcast entities, technical standards, and adherence to broadcasting codes.

Postal Services

Universal postal service provisions, licensing of postal operators, and standards for mail delivery services.

Internet

Internet governance, cybersecurity oversight, domain registry management, and protection of internet infrastructure.
